Executive Director, Bonavista International School

Mrs. Morawo attended Ilaro Polytechnic for her ND program in Food Science and Technology in 1990, and proceeded to Yaba College of Education where she obtained her HND in 1993. Mrs. Morawo went into business immediately after her marriage supporting their beautiful children and husband. Mrs. Morawo left with her husband to Canada with their children in 2004, and while in Canada attended George Brown college, Toronto , Ontario, where she studied Food and Nutrition, She also attended Bow valley college College, Calgary AB, where she bagged diploma in Human Resources Management. Mrs. Morawo loves working with children, hence she obtained a diploma in Early Childhood Education (ECE) in Calgary, Alberta. While in Canada, she worked with Calgary Board of Education, Calgary Library and Canada post.

Mrs. Morawo did a lot of volunteering work and among the places she volunteered were: Weston Park Baptist Church, Toronto, Canada; Senior community Center, Calgary, Canada. Mrs. Morawo together with her husband and children are working together to realize their long term dream of touching lives through the establishment of Bonavista International School.
