Welcome Adddress

Success Garden international Schools which comprises a nursery, primary and secondary is located okwuabala Ihioma, Orlu L.G.A, Imo State, Nigeria. Success Garden International School is a training college for children. We train them academically, spiritually, and physically, fully prepared for secondary education as caring, confident, independent, resilient, well rounded individuals with a real love for learning.

The bible says “train up a child in the way he should go so that when he is old, he will not depart from it”. At Success Garden International Schools, we have specialized subject teachers for French, Computers, Drama, Music, Sports, Science, etc. Our computer room allows our primary school children access to technology from an early age.

OBJECTIVES OF SUCCESS GARDEN INTERNATIONAL SCHOOLS


To provide an atmosphere that promotes respect and concern for all individuals

To provide stimulating materials and guidance in their use of materials that will enable each child to realize his/her full potential.

To encourage rational thinking, fair play, self reliance and individual freedom and responsibility.

To provide opportunities for exploration and creativity.

To respect each child’s individuality and promote attitudes, interests, and understanding which will enable him/her to be happy, secure, and contributing member of society
