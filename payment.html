Fees and Payment

Fees will be sent home with the pupils' report card at the end of each term. Fees are to be paid one week before the resumption of a new term.

Discount Policy

  • A third child from the same parent will receive 10% discount on school fee only.
  • The forth, fifth and more children from the same parents will receive 15% discount on school fee only.
