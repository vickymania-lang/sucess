About Us
Garden international Schools which comprises of Success Garden international Secondary School and Success Garden Montessori Academy (pre-school, creche, nursery, and primary) is located in the city of Okwuabala Ihioma, Orlu L.G.A , Imo State, Nigeria,was established on the 5th of September,2016 to raise academic giants. Success Garden international Schools are international training institutions for pupils and students of the 21st century. We train them in financial management, academics, spiritual and physical aspect for effective global competition fully prepared for lower and higher education as caring, confident, independent, resilient, well rounded individuals with a real love for learning.

The bible says: train up a child in the way he should go so that when he is old, he will not depart from it. At Success Garden international Schools we have specialized subject teachers for French, Computers, Drama, Music, Sports, Science, etc. Our computer room allows our primary school children access to technology from an early age. The different learning equipment promotes hands on self-paced learning. Small classes allow the teacher to really get to know each of her pupils intimately and work with them both individually and in small groups. You will never regret bringing your child/ward to Success Garden Montessori Academy and Success Garden international Secondary School..

I would like to implore everyone, the students, teachers, and parents to join hands together to make the school a point of reference where no child is left behind and no one is cast out because of his/her family or financial background as we are all equal before God. Thank you and God bless you!

Mr. Okwuma Godson Osborn (LLB) Proprietor
