Curriculum

Success Garden School operates a uniquely powerful curriculum.  We combined the Federal Government of Nigeria education curriculum with Canadian education curriculum to form a rich curriculum that prepares our pupils for the Nigeria national common entrance examination and any other examinations around the world.

Our programmes are powered by relevant and exotic learning resources; delivered through diverse methodologies.

The result – Success Garden School children have an in-depth knowledge in varieties of topics and can discuss intelligently on various subjects and relate socially and conduct themselves acceptably anywhere they find themselves.

We, at Bonavista School can safely say, “we run a curriculum that empowers children with critical thinking skills”.
